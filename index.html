Climate science question answering

Grounded question answering over about 3,000 climate science papers from arXiv. Retrieval uses BM25 with HyDE query expansion and a language-model reranker, and uses no dense embedders. Answers carry inline citations and decline when the corpus does not cover the question, and the system ships with a two-tier evaluation.

Faithfulness of 0.947, with CPU-only retrieval performing on par with a GPU embedder.

Antibiotic discovery

A multi-task graph neural network predicts antibacterial potency. A reinforcement-learning agent then generates new candidate molecules guided by those predictions, benchmarked against four baseline generators.

AUROC of 0.83 and 0.87 on the two targets, and 20,031 unique valid molecules generated.

Drug-interaction benchmark audit

A methodology audit of a multi-task drug-interaction benchmark. Seven model variants are used to show where standard benchmark-assembly choices quietly disadvantage tuberculosis and HIV drugs, and the audit finds and corrects a label-leakage bug along the way.

A simple multilayer perceptron beats the graph neural network by 9.3 accuracy points, a gap that is statistically significant under McNemar's test.

Tuberculosis drug-resistance support

A hybrid system for reasoning about tuberculosis drug resistance. It pairs a WHO-catalog knowledge graph and a symbolic rule engine with a case-based layer for situations the rules do not cover, alongside a natural-language query layer. Every classification traces back to a specific catalog rule, which keeps the reasoning auditable and explainable.

99.9 percent catalog fidelity, validated on 13,118 real isolates from the CRyPTIC consortium.

Risk-controlled MRI reconstruction

Accelerated low-field MRI reconstruction with per-slice risk control. A lightweight variational network reconstructs each slice, and an empirical-Bernstein gate then certifies which slices are trustworthy and flags the rest.

Per-slice certification of reconstructions through an empirical-Bernstein trust gate.
